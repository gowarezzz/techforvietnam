Program overview

Program overview

Tech for Vietnam is an initiative aimed at training 1,000 product management talents by 2025 and enhancing the professional skills of Vietnam’s workforce to better meet the rising demands of the global job market through the collaborative efforts of strategic partners.

Built on insights from over 20 Product management meetups, our program address the real needs and challenges faced by both current and aspiring product managers, product team leaders or other senior managers and executives that have responsibility for the organization’s product and service offerings.

Varying PM standards

Different companies use different product management methods, making it hard for product managers to adapt quickly. This lack of consistency can lead to confusion and inefficiency, slowing down the path to successful product delivery.

Limited mentorship and growth opportunities
