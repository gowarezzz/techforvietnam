New Product Challenge for Aspiring Entrepreneurs
Did you know that over 80 percent of new products fail? Don't let your entrepreneurial dream become another statistic. Join us for an immersive eight-hour workshop designed to equip aspiring entrepreneurs with the tools and knowledge to turn their ideas into successful products.


Who Will Benefit?

This workshop is ideal for aspiring entrepreneurs who are ready to enhance their chances of success. Whether you're nurturing a promising idea, recovering from a setback, or seeking the right opportunity, this workshop is tailored to meet your needs.

Participants will achieve the most benefit from this workshop if they already have a potential market opportunity in mind and are able to apply the workshop concepts to this opportunity.


What Participants Will Learn?

Understand the common causes of product failures and the key principles of successful products. Explore a proven step-by-step process to:

  • Identify and validate high-potential market needs.

  • Develop products and services that resonate with customers.

  • Craft a clear market strategy and acquire your first customers.

  • Get ready for a highly interactive experience where you'll apply concepts to your own idea, engage in discussions with fellow entrepreneurs, and gain insights from real-world examples.

Our training fosters collaboration and encourages participants to share their experiences and learn from one another.

Key Concepts

  • Identifying new market opportunities using Jobs-to-Be-Done framework.

  • Leveraging segmentation and personas to understand customer needs.

  • Validating market opportunity hypotheses through iterative processes.

  • Applying Design Thinking principles for innovation.

  • Evaluating potential business models and defining your unique value proposition.

  • Iterating towards achieving Product-Market Fit.

Don't miss this opportunity to transform your entrepreneurial journey. Enroll now and take the first step towards turning your vision into reality.


Trainer Profile

Tom Evans is Principal at The Lûcrum Group, Strategic Advisor at Productside and Board Member of ProductCamp Austin. He is recognized globally for his expertise in product management, crafting effective go-to-market strategies, facilitating global market entry, and spearheading new product development initiatives. With a wealth of experience spanning startups to Global 2000 enterprises, Tom has consistently empowered organizations to conceive and launch successful products.

His extensive portfolio crosses diverse industries, technologies, and international markets, cementing his reputation as a sought-after consultant. Tom's impactful guidance has transformed companies across various sectors including software, electronics, financial services, healthcare, professional services, manufactured goods, industrial products, and telecommunications. Tom's expertise extends across continents, having conducted workshops, delivered keynote addresses, and provided strategic insights at industry gatherings throughout the Americas, Middle East, Africa, Western Europe, Southeast Asia, and Australia.
